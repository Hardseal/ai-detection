# 3.1.1: Limit system access to authorized users

This control is implemented through a combination of policy, procedure,
and technical configuration. The organization has documented the
implementation in the System Security Plan. Evidence of implementation
is tracked in our POA&M and reviewed annually by the responsible role.
Controls are reviewed quarterly and all findings are remediated according
to severity in accordance with NIST SP 800-171 and CMMC Level 2
assessment objectives. Shared responsibility with our external service
provider is documented in the customer responsibility matrix.

## Implementation specifics: Widget Defense Corp

Access to systems that store, process, or transmit CUI is gated by Okta
Single Sign-On (tenant widgetdefense.okta.com). All administrators
authenticate with YubiKey FIDO2 hardware tokens; TOTP fallback is
disabled. Group-based entitlements are managed in Entra ID security
groups prefixed CUI- and are synchronized to Okta every 15 minutes by
SCIM job scim_okta_entra_v3.

The ISSO (Janelle Ruiz, janelle.ruiz@widgetdefense.com) runs the
quarterly access review using Splunk saved search
`svc_entitlement_review_quarterly` which exports to
`\\\\fileserver01\\compliance\\access-reviews\\2026-Q1.xlsx`. The
Q1-2026 review closed on 2026-03-18 with four entitlements revoked and
one exception (service account `svc-backup-prod`) with approval memo
MEMO-2026-019 expiring 2026-06-18.

Evidence: access-review-2026Q1.xlsx, okta-admin-roster-2026-03-18.csv,
memo MEMO-2026-019.
