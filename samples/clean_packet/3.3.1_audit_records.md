# 3.3.1: Audit and Accountability Narrative

Splunk Enterprise indexes syslog from every in-scope endpoint, router, switch, and cloud control plane. Azure Monitor and CloudTrail feeds are normalized through a Cribl pipeline before ingestion. Retention is 12 months hot, 7 years cold in an AES-256-encrypted S3 bucket with object-lock and MFA-delete.

Event types captured include authentication, authorization, privilege escalation, process creation, network connection, and policy change. Each event is hashed SHA-256 at ingest; the hash chain is re-verified nightly by a stdlib Python job owned by the ISSO.

Review cadence: daily for alert triage, quarterly for coverage audit. The responsible role is the ISSO, with the sysadmin as backup. Evidence of coverage: Splunk dashboard `audit_coverage` exported monthly.
